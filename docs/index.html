Comprehensive management tools for running payroll, configuring settings, tracking attendance, and managing classroom economies.

Automated Payroll

Configure pay rates, schedules, and automatic rewards or fines. Let the system handle the calculations while you focus on teaching.

Virtual and physical items with bundles, expirations, and redemption tracking. Students learn budgeting and purchasing decisions.

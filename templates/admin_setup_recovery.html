Setup Account Recovery

Classroom Token Hub uses a unique account recovery method for teacher users. Instead of collecting sensitive personal inforamtion such as email addresses or phone number, we utilizes a student-assisted recovery process.

Account recover would require two pieces of unique information:

  • The sum of your date of birth (something you know)
  • Your student usernames from the classes you teach (something you have)

Because your account was created before this new feature, you would need to provide the sum of your date of birth to enable this recovery option.

Your date of birth sum will be processed and stored securely. The processed value cannot be reversed to recover the original value.

Calculate: Month (1-12) + Day (1-31) + Year (4 digits)
